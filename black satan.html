You fester in my wounds of nine,

​	For you are the Lord of maggots.

Worms, wretched serpents sown in soil, eat your fill

​	And enmesh my essence in the earth.

POETRY FOR THE DEAD MAN.

Hear this cry, hare hare

Hare, a thousand times hare.

The womb of the earth is lying quiet,
The seed of the sky is standing bare.
I am standing at the rim,
I am standing at the fore.
The mask of my face is frowning down.
I am crying in fear.
